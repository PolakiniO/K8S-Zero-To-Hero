# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This repository is a public learning resource. Contributors are expected to keep discussion respectful, technically grounded, and safe for a broad audience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and review comments.
- Focus criticism on the content or implementation, not on people.
- Avoid harassment, insults, discrimination, or doxxing.
- Do not post secrets, private infrastructure details, customer data, or private workspace exports.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, stalking, or personal attacks.
- Publishing someone else's private information without consent.
- Posting credentials, kubeconfigs, cloud keys, browser exports, or internal documents.
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this code of conduct or creates publication risk for the repository.

## Reporting

Report serious conduct or publication-safety concerns privately to the maintainer instead of opening a public issue.
